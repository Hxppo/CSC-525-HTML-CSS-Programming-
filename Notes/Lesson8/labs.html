"list-style-type" property defines the style of the list item marker.

disc - lists item marker to a bullet (default)
circle - lists item marker to a circle
square - lists item marker to a square
none - lists item not to be marked

Table Format (rowspan attribute)

Week 1 Cost Savings for holiday!
Week 2 $1200 $2600
Week 3 $500
Week 3 $900
<table> <tr> <th>Week 1</th> <th>Cost</th> <th>Savings for holiday!</th> </tr> <tr> <td>Week 2</td> <td>$1200</td> <td rowspan="3">$2600</td> </tr> <tr> <td>Week 3</td> <td>$500</td> </tr> <tr> <td>Week 3</td> <td>$900</td> </tr> </table>
